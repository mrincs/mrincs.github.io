About Me

As an avid enthusiast in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, I take interest in understanding the recent developments and state-of-art researches in the domain across industry and academia as well as understanding the effects it will cause on our lives in future. I have spent significant time in both academia and industries and built up my experiences on both front: as a researcher and an engineers. My belief is research and engineering are closely associated together. Research in sciences enables us to build a prototype of future advancements whereas science without engineering is just a philosophy. Besides my primary interests, I also love to paint, keep aware of foreign policies and read fictions and non-fictions in my spare time.
